Inspired by the bots created by Internet artist, Darius Kazemi , I wanted to learn about building and deploying Twitter bots.

Pick Two Bot, my first bot, is based on the project management triangle: "good, fast, cheap: pick two". The bot queries Twitter for a random recent tweet that includes three nouns and reformats them in the following way: "{noun}. {noun}. {noun}. Pick two." Below is an example.

The bot uses the Worknik API to identify the parts of speech in a given tweet and then formats them in the singular form.

The bot runs as a Node.js app as a worker on Heroku and fails silently if it doesn't find a Tweet with 3 nouns. It just tries again in the next time interval.
